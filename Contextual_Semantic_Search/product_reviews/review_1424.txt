The Sandstone Traditional Arctic quilt coat is made of high quality-durable fabric. It is water resistant and extremely warm against harsh winter weather. I like how Carhartt sticks to their line of more casual style of clothing and not creating something shiny,fancy and bold. This coat will keep you warm enough, so that you will not have to dress in too many layers. Carhartt makes clothing for the hard-working man. This coat is what we call "A Man's Coat".
