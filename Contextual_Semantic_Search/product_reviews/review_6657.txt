My best friend calls them my tap shoes and she doesn't believe they're real heels. But they are the most comfortable heels you will ever buy. CAUTION the nail in the heel doesn't wear down as fast as the rest of the heel so if you step wrong your heel can slip (surprisingly hard to do). I can't blaim the shoe, they are meant to be tap shoes, but if you wear them as heels, just know. This tends to happen on linoleum floors and I've never actually fallen
